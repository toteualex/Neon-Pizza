The Place

Neon Pizza is a welcoming and cozy family owned restaurant established in 2001.
In a modern setting restaurant we serve our delicious traditional pizza, authentic, flavorful, using a century old technique and always the freshest ingredients.
We cook all our pizzas in a wood fire over which ensures the best taste.
Also be careful and make sure you save some room for our incredible desserts.
We are open and ready to delight you with our treats seven days a week.

We are OPEN daily !
